# Feedback And Corrections

This layer is a published, reviewed derivative of a private knowledge base. It is not the canonical source, and it is not actively developed as an open collaborative project — but corrections and feedback are welcome.

## How To Give Feedback

- If this content is hosted somewhere with an issue tracker, open an issue describing the correction, question, or suggestion.
- Otherwise, use info@thelastech.com to reach the maintainer.

## What's Useful To Report

- Factual errors or outdated information.
- Unclear or ambiguous explanations.
- Broken links or references.
- Ideas for additional patterns worth documenting.

## What This Isn't

- Not a place to request private/internal details — this layer intentionally excludes operational, credential, and client-sensitive material.
- Not a guarantee of response time or acceptance of proposed changes; all edits are reviewed and rewritten by the maintainer before publication.

Thank you for reading and for any corrections you send along.
